FAQ

Fair questions.

When do I actually pay?

Never at request time. Once we confirm a checker for a real time slot, you place a card hold — authorised, not charged. The charge only happens when your report is delivered. No report within 24 hours: the hold releases automatically and you pay nothing.

What if the landlord refuses the viewing?

That is an answer — refusal plus pressure to pay fast is the single biggest scam signal there is. It goes in your report as a finding, with everything else we verified from records.

What if it turns out to be a scam?

Then the check just did its job — you keep your bond. Around half of the checks that find red flags never even need a visit: the background research catches it first.

Is this a guarantee?

No — and anyone selling you a guarantee is lying. We report what we verify and observe at a point in time, with timestamped evidence, and the decision stays yours. We'll also remind you of the safe way to pay: in NZ, bonds go through Tenancy Services — never "held" by bank transfer.

Which cities do you cover?

We're starting in one city and expanding by waitlist demand. If yours isn't covered yet, your request puts you first in line the moment it is.
